Little Bit About Us

BookMyShow was founded by Ashish Hemrajani, Parikshit Dar, and Rajesh Balpande, all alumni of Sydenham Institute of Management, Mumbai University. They had been working for J.W. Thompson, SPAR, and Chatterjee Soros Group, respectively. In 2007 they officially launched the BookMyShow website with the goal of introducing online movie ticketing to India.
